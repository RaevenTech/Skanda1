How We Do It

Remote Development; How It Will Work For You

We offer a remote development service based in South Africa, we offer lower costs for development when compared to companies based in Europe, the United Kingdom or the United States while offering a highly skilled workforce of developers across all technologies. We build teams based on the needs of our clients. We will structure the teams based on the project size and budget, ensuring that the correct skills and capabilities are assigned to each client. In all cases the projects are managed by competent, experienced project managers assigned to be the key point of interaction. We offer the full range of skills from Business Analysis to development, to ensure effective project delivery. Once a project is delivered, we offer full support for the product at the scale necessary. We can offer full support of dedicated developers to ensure that the application is functioning effectively at all times. We currently carry this out for large retailers and other customers. We have a custom fault logging system that ensures no issues are unaddressed.

“Enterprise Architecture; How It Will Work For You

In the Enterprise Architecture consulting area, we offer experienced Enterprise Architects who can help with establishing the correct Enterprise Architecture for your organisation based on the current state and the desired future state, we specialise in giving an unbiased roadmap to achieving the correct future architecture because we are not linked to any software vendors. We will deliver an architecture that is entirely uninfluenced by vendor specific issues but will accommodate the current products in your company’s architecture. We have worked at the level of the state railways in South Africa and can handle large architecture projects.
